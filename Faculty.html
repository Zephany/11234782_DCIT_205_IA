About

I have over a decade of experience instructing and lecturing on various computer science topics at the university level. I have also held positions as an IT manager and general manager, leading teams in the technology departments of multiple organizations. I have extensive experience with Data Science, Machine Learning, and Artificial Intelligence, and I am certified by organizations such as IBM. I have completed several projects in this field. In addition to my technical expertise, I am proficient with several industry tools, such as Jira, the Microsoft Azure DevOps board, GitHub, Bitbucket, Power BI, and Tableau. I have extensive experience producing high-quality technological solutions and leading development teams utilizing agile project management techniques. Some of my students have obtained internships with Fortune 500 companies, including Goldman Sachs, Facebook Meta, and Google Germany. I believe I possess a wealth of experience and skills in the industry, a track record of successfully completing projects, and the ability to lead and mentor others effectively.
